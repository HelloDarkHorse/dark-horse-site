Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025  ·  Last Updated: January 2026

Dark Horse is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with Missouri state laws and applicable privacy regulations.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to operate and improve our services:

  • Automatically Collected Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and access times to help us manage and secure our website.
  • Voluntarily Provided Data: When you contact us or use our services, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, and business-related information.

We do not collect or use mobile phone numbers for marketing or promotional purposes unless explicitly provided for service-related communication.

Use of Collected Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Deliver and improve our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Maintain security and detect/prevent fraud
  • Improve website functionality and user experience

No mobile information will be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except for aggregators and providers of the text message services.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These do not collect personal information and may be disabled in your browser settings.

Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website may include links to external sites for your convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites. Visiting external sites is at your discretion.

User-Generated Content & Feedback

Feedback or content submitted to Dark Horse may be used to improve our services or support communications. No personal data will be shared without your consent.

Data Protection & Security

We implement commercially reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. While no online method is 100% secure, we actively monitor and update our systems.

Your Rights Under Missouri Privacy Laws

As a Missouri resident, you have the right to:

  • Access and Correct your personal information
  • Request Deletion of your data when it is no longer needed
  • Opt-Out of non-service-related communications

To exercise these rights, please contact kat@darkhorseva.net.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.

For questions or concerns regarding this policy, contact kat@darkhorseva.net.
