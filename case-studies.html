Facebook Ads, Workflow Automation & VA Integration

Client: Angie H., Texas Engagement: ~18 months

"Having her on my team has been life changing... it's lightened our load and improved our efficiencies tremendously."

— Angie H., Texas

The Challenge

Angie came in already tech savvy with some systems in place — she wasn't starting from zero. But having a foundation and having a fully optimized, running operation are two different things. She needed someone who could meet her where she was, build on what already existed, and take the time-consuming operational pieces entirely off her plate.

What We Did

I expanded her existing GoHighLevel setup — layering in additional workflows, pipelines, automations, and email sequences. I ran targeted Facebook ad campaigns to fill the top of the funnel, then embedded a dedicated VA into the operation to handle inbound follow-up and convert prospects into booked appointments.

Consistent new bookings handled entirely by VA — no owner involvement required

Owner fully removed from lead management — back to doing the work, not chasing leads

Full GHL Build + Ad Rebuild + Staff Training

Cut Lead Costs by 90%. Closed Half the Room.

Client: Eric Schneider, Ohio

"The process has been incredibly smooth. She has delivered on all fronts — dedicated, knowledgeable, and results driven."

— Eric S., Ohio

The Challenge

The client was running Facebook ads and paying $12–$20 per lead with inconsistent results. The leads they did get weren't converting — staff had no defined follow-up process, no system to manage inquiries, and no training on how to move a prospect to a booked appointment. The problem wasn't awareness. It was operations.

What We Did

I delivered a complete GoHighLevel build — workflows, pipelines, automations, email sequences, and funnels — plus website updates to align with the new funnel flow. I rebuilt the Facebook ad campaigns from the ground up. Then I trained the staff directly inside GHL: how to respond to leads, what to say, and how to move a prospect from first inquiry to booked appointment.

Results

Cost per lead dropped from $12–$20 down to $1–$2 — a 90%+ reduction

~50% lead-to-booking conversion rate achieved after staff training

Staff operating confidently inside GHL with no ongoing hand-holding required
